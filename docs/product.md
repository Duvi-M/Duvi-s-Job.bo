# Product

## Vision

Duvi's Job.bo is a job marketplace for Bolivia that makes hiring more trustworthy and easier to navigate for candidates and companies.

The MVP focuses on a clean foundation for verified job posts, simple applications, company profiles, and future admin review workflows.

## User Roles

- `candidate`: searches jobs, manages a candidate profile, and applies to openings.
- `company_user`: drafts and manages job openings for a company.
- `company_admin`: manages company users, company profile, and published jobs.
- `platform_admin`: reviews companies, jobs, and platform activity.

## MVP Modules

- `auth`: accounts, sessions, role-based access.
- `candidates`: candidate profiles and basic resume data.
- `companies`: company profiles and verification status.
- `jobs`: job creation, publishing, listing, and detail pages.
- `applications`: candidate applications to jobs.
- `admin`: platform review tools for companies and jobs.

## Future Modules

- CV parser
- Job-candidate matching
- Recommendations
- Notifications
- Analytics
